Why Learn Chess Online?

Benefits of Learning Chess Online

You don’t have to travel to go to a training centre. You just have to login and enter the online classroom. A coach comes online at a time that suits the student. You can learn from the comfort of your own home. Online Coaching is available in different time zones for different countries.

This is real time coaching in our online classroom where a student is trained by our coach in live and interactive sessions. This direct interaction helps the student to learn faster and more effectively compared to pre recorded sessions of classes. Here you can talk and interact with your coach and ask him whatever doubts you have and you will get a reply immediately.



In traditional training methods, you have to spend time in reaching a training centre or if a coach comes to your place, it will add to the amount you pay because he spends time to reach your place. Thus choosing online coaching is more affordable and it saves a lot of your time.

We not only conduct training sessions, but in addition to that we have weekly online chess tournaments among the students with attractive prizes and certificates!



Since Chess is a mental game unlike other physical games, there isn’t much difference between online mode and offline mode of training. So why waste your precious time and energy going for the offline classes!
